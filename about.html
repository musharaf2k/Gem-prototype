Our Story

Three generations of expertise in rare gemstones

Heritage & Excellence

Since 1952, the GEMSHIRE legacy has been built on an unwavering commitment to sourcing the world's most exceptional gemstones.

Founded by master gemologist Thomas Ashworth in the heart of London's diamond district, GEMSHIRE began as a small family business with an extraordinary vision: to make the world's most beautiful and ethically sourced gemstones accessible to discerning collectors worldwide.

What started as a single storefront has evolved into a global network of gemstone experts, miners, and craftspeople, all united by our shared passion for exceptional quality and ethical practices. Today, we continue to honor Thomas's original vision while embracing modern innovations in gemstone sourcing and certification.

Our third-generation leadership combines traditional expertise with cutting-edge technology, ensuring every stone in our collection meets the highest standards of beauty, authenticity, and ethical provenance.
